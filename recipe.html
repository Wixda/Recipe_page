Classic Pancake Recipe

Fluffy, golden pancakes perfect for breakfast or brunch. Easy to make and delicious!

Source: Link to the Site

Recipe Information

  • Preparation time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 4
  • Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 large egg
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, melted butter, and egg.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix!
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and grease lightly.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake onto the skillet.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.

Tips

  • Do not overmix the batter for fluffy pancakes.
  • Add chocolate chips or blueberries for extra flavor.
  • Let the batter rest for a few minutes before cooking.
